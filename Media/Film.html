TV Shows


Bojack Horseman

BoJack Horseman is considered a good show by many for a variety of reasons. One reason is its clever writing, which blends humor and tragedy in a way that can be both entertaining and emotionally impactful. The show also tackles a variety of complex and relevant themes, such as mental health, addiction, fame, and relationships, in a thoughtful and nuanced manner. Additionally, the show features a talented voice cast and unique animation style that sets it apart from other animated series. Overall, BoJack Horseman has been praised for its depth, humor, and willingness to explore difficult topics in a mature and engaging way.


Cuphead Show

Cuphead is a well-received and popular show for several reasons. Its unique animation style and the nods to classic cartoons of the 1930s has garnered praise from both fans and critics alike. Additionally, the show's plot and characters are engaging and offer viewers a fresh take on the world of video games. The show's creators have also done an excellent job of bringing the game's characters to life in the show, making it a must-watch for fans of the game. Overall, Cuphead is a great example of how video game adaptations can be successful in a different medium and offer fans new ways to enjoy their favorite franchises.

Howl's Moving Castle

"Howl's Moving Castle" is considered a great film for a multitude of reasons, including its stunning animation, memorable characters, compelling storyline, and timeless themes. It is a film that has captured the hearts of audiences around the world, both young and old, and continues to be a beloved classic in the world of animation. Its influence can be seen in the works of other filmmakers and animators, and it has solidified its place in the canon of great films. Whether you are a fan of animation, fantasy, or simply a good story, "Howl's Moving Castle" is definitely worth a watch.


Shrek 2

Shrek 2 is another great movie because it manages to take everything that made the original film so special and elevate it to a new level. The animation is more stunning, the jokes are funnier, and the characters are more fully realized. The storyline is engaging and features themes of self-discovery, acceptance, and love. Plus, the addition of memorable new characters like Puss in Boots and the Fairy Godmother add even more depth and humor to the already rich world of Shrek. All of these elements make Shrek 2 not only a great sequel, but a great movie in its own right.
